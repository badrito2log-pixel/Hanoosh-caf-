About Hanoosh Café

At Hanoosh Café, we believe that food and coffee are more than nourishment — they are experiences meant to be savored. Each dish and drink is crafted with care, attention to detail, and deep respect for flavor.

Our menu blends tradition with creativity, drawing inspiration from global flavors and local Somali favorites. Beyond food, Hanoosh is a place to pause, breathe, and belong.
